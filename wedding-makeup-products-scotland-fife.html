Makeup by Karen

 


As with most professional makeup artists my kit contains a variety of products all from different cosmetic houses such as Chanel, Lancome, Nars, Dior, YSL, Mac, Clinique, Laura Mercier ect.

My must have products are:
Laura Mercier foundation Primer - this enables foundation to sit on a smooth base allowing your makeup to look fresh all day.
Lancome skin smoothing concealer
Mac Strobe Cream - to add illumination to the skin.
YSL Touch Eclat - to add radiance and brighten under the eyes.
Laura Mercier Secret Brightening Powder - normal transluscent powder is too heavy for under the eyes and can be ageing, this powder smooths and brightens the skin under the eyes.
Mac eyepaints - to help hold the eyeshadow in place and to assist in the blending to create a smokey eye.

If you have any favourite products you wish me to use then I am happy to do so.

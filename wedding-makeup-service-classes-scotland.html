Makeup by Karen

 


Makeup Trial - This involves an indepth consultation looking over your ideas, dress, hairstyle, colour scheme etc.  This enables me to get a feel for the look or theme you wish to achieve.  This can take a few hours as I will offer hints and tips and only leave when you are completely satisfied with your makeup. I believe that I am not only making up a face but I am also making up a person which is why a trial is so important.

Wedding day - A minimum of 4 people (This price includes the application of individual eyelash extensions).  I will come to your chosen location and arrive in time to get set up.  I will stay with you until you are ready to leave and I always refresh previous makeups to ensure everyone looks perfect.  I do request a deposit of £30 to secure bookings.  Confirmation will be sent out on receipt of the deposit which will be deducted from the final balance.

Makeup lessons - Having taught fashion and photographic makeup I now offer my services to those wishing to know how to apply their own makeup.

Please note that all deposits are non refundable. I charge 50p/mile for travel expenses out with a 15 mile radius.
